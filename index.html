What is Fuelr?

We manage your fuel log so that you don't have to. Our system will automatically calculate the numbers that you put in, keeping an up-to-date log and cool statistics.

As a motorist, you rely heavily on fuel. When the price of fuel goes up, you need to know how that affects you and your vehicle. You want to know how much fuel your car consumes. Once you know this, you can work on finding ways to lower your consumption or find another vehicle that gives you better mileage.

What can fuelr do?

  • Track fuel slips
  • Automatically calculate average fuel consumption (overall and per fill up)
  • Keep a log of fuel-ups
  • Manage multiple cars per user
  • Crafted for use on mobile smartphones and tablets
